Who We Are

More than a brand
a way of life

Nyuma Yenu is a Nairobi-born lifestyle collective that started as an umbrella for creative and experiential ventures from catering tables to game nights, from event stages to design studios.

Today, we have simplified our focus to what matters most: joy, movement, good food, and the kind of happiness you feel in your chest. We're the people behind your best memories.

Our Vision

A world that chooses joy, daily.

To be East Africa's most loved lifestyle group the name people think of when they want to live better, laugh louder, and experience more.

Our Mission

We engineer happiness.

To create experiences, spaces, and stories that make people feel fully alive through food, travel, play, and genuine human connection.

Our Promise

Behind every great moment, we're there.

Whatever the occasion, whenever you need us, Nyuma Yenu shows up thoughtful, warm, and completely dedicated to making it unforgettable.
